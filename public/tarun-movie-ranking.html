The Best Movies According to Tarun

My top 3 movies of all-time.

My favorite telugu movie. I love a lot of things in this movie like its songs, visuals, story, screenplay and acting.

Kimi No Na Wa (Your Name)

My favorite anime movie. I really enjoyed its visuals and Story
